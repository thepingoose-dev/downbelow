# Privacy Policy

## Overview
This game does not collect, store, or transmit personal data from players.

We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency regarding how
information is handled.

---

## Data Collection
The game does **not**:
- Collect personal information
- Require user accounts
- Track user behavior
- Use analytics services
- Store cookies
- Transmit data to external servers

---

## Third-Party Services
The game does not integrate with third-party services that collect or process
personal data.

If this changes in the future, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.

---

## Children’s Privacy
This game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under
the age of 13.

---

## Data Security
Because no personal data is collected or stored, no personal data is retained
or processed by the game.

---

## Changes to This Policy
This policy may be updated if the game’s features change.

The most current version will always be available at this page.

## Contact

through the official community channels listed on the game’s Steam page.
