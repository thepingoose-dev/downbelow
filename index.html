About the Game

Step into a mysterious afterlife where religion, myth, and legend intertwine. Race toward the legendary city of Agoralopis while the Oblivion closes in.

Explore a pixel world filled with characters, choices, and hidden lore. Combat erupts suddenly — fast, intense arena encounters where survival depends on movement and awareness.

Key Features

  • 🕯 Narrative-driven story with meaningful choices
  • ⚔ Hybrid gameplay: exploration + arena combat
  • 🌑 Dark atmospheric world inspired by myth and religion
  • 🧠 Dialogue and decisions shape your journey
